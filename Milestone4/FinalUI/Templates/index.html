Easy Plant Disease Detection Guide

User Guidance

Follow the steps below to properly use the plant disease detection tool:

  1. Step 1: Upload a clear image of the plant leaf by clicking the 'Upload Image' button.
  2. Step 2: Review the preview of the uploaded image.
  3. Step 3: Click 'Analyze' to start the analysis process.
  4. Step 4: Wait for the progress bar to complete.
  5. Step 5: View the disease name detected and recommended cure.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What kind of images can I upload?

You can upload clear, high-resolution images of plant leaves. Make sure the leaf is not blurry and is well-lit to ensure accurate analysis.

How long does it take for the analysis to complete?

The analysis typically takes a few seconds depending on the image size and complexity of the leaf disease.

Can I upload multiple images at once?

Currently, the tool supports one image upload at a time. Please upload one image and wait for the results before uploading another.

What should I do if the tool doesn't detect a disease?

If the tool doesn't detect a disease, try uploading a clearer image or one with better lighting. If the issue persists, it may indicate that the leaf is healthy or the tool has limitations in detecting certain diseases.
